The day before MageTitans, on the 12th of septeber Elgentos is organizing a Hyva Hackathon! At this event, we will work in teams on problems and/or features within the Hyva ecosystem. The goal here is twofold. We aim to improve the product and allow participants to gain technical knowledge by delving deeper.

It is a great opportunity to work on a nice feature with colleagues from other organizations.

Of course, Elgentos provides everything you need for a productive workday. Just make sure you bring a laptop with an installed version of Magento with the sample data.

Location: elgentos office, Hereweg 120-4, 9725AK, Groningen
Time: 9:00 - 17:30
Lunch: Will be provided, we will gather your preferences and dietary restrictions in the morning.

Ideas can be submitted in advance using this form.

Tickets for this event are free and can be ordered here.
