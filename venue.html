So why Groningen?

Why are we hosting this event in Groningen? Well, as many of you know, Groningen is the Magento capital of the Netherlands. We have the most certified developers of any Dutch city. We even rank 4th worldwide according to research by [Inchoo](https://inchoo.net)! Besides this, Groningen is a beautiful and quaint student city with a population of 200.000 (including over 30.000 students!). Here are [more great facts about our great city](https://www.cityoftalent.nl/en/city-of-talent/life/25-facts-about-groningen). Now you know why.
